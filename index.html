Food I Like in New York

  • somtum der

    • Somtum Der specialized in authentic, yet contemporary, Isan (Northeastern Thai)cuisine. Their Pad Ki Mao is my favorite.

  • Kimura

    • Kimura is a Japanese restaurant specializing in motsunabe(a traditional japanese hotpot), a rich broth made of beef or pork intestines. If you are uncomfortable with organ meats, they also provide shabu-shabu, rice bowls, and sushi. My favorite item here is the Collage Nabe.

  • Pocha 32

    • POCHA means a vendor in a street or other public places that serves Korean comfort food or market food. I had many happy memories here after a night out with friends. Their watermelon soju is very famous. To me, nothing beats a late night seafood jeongol.

  • Miriam

    • Miriam serves uniquely, distinctly Israeli cuisine. Their menu is seasonal, and many of the ingredients come straight from Israel. As a big fan of mediterranean food, I love every dish here, but Kadaif Wrapped Shrimp and Lamb Shawarma are probably my favorite.
